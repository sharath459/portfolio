Why Hire Me? My Philosophy on Data

The Core Need

I believe the primary role of BI is to deliver efficient, accurate, and reliable answers to business questions. Whether it's to prioritize milestones, quantify defects, measure success, or justify funding for new projects, data must provide a clear path forward.

My Solution-Oriented Products

To meet these needs, I focus on building a suite of customer-facing BI products:

  • Dashboards & Decks: For standardized metrics at everyone's fingertips.
  • Query Banks: For deep-dives into frequently asked questions.
  • Simplified Data Tables: For ad-hoc analysis that goes beyond pre-defined metrics.

The End-to-End Data Journey

I understand the entire data lifecycle, from initial instrumentation by engineering teams, through data pipelining and warehousing, to the creation of a meaningful front-end with insightful business context. This holistic view allows me to build robust, scalable, and truly self-service data solutions that empower organizations.
